Fernando is a self-driven person with a passion for learning and anything related to computer science and software development.
Over the years he has created and developed several free software projects and collaborated with some others. Some examples of these projects are a BitTorrent client or a wireless intrusion detection system.
He is currently working as a data scientist at Momentum, a KDDI subsidiary company. In the past, he also has worked at several universities and research institutions, both in Spain and in Japan, and as a result, has published several papers. His research interests lie primarily in the area of deductive databases, although he has worked in other areas, like natural language processing.
